YG Entertainment released a statement sharing, “We checked with Nam Joo Hyuk
and Lee Sung Kyung, and it is true that the two recently broke up,”
and added that the exact reason cannot be revealed as it is their
personal matter.
On August 18, it was revealed that Lee Sung Kyung and
Nam Joo Hyuk have reportedly broken up.
According to industry representatives,
they naturally grew apart due to busy schedules and
have decided to maintain a supportive senior-junior relationship.
In response, a source from their agency
YG Entertainment commented, “We will release a statement after checking.”
